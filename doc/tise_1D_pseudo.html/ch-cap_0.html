Chapter 1 - Introduction

1.1 What's tise_1D_pseudo?

The Fortran 90 programs and Perl scripts in the tise_1D_pseudo suite solve the one dimensional time-independent Schroedinger equation for a one body problem and for several potentials using a expansion in three possible bases: harmonic oscillator (HO), transformed harmonic oscillator (THO), and infinite square well box (BOX). The full spectrum and eigenstates are computed. This includes both the (negative energy) bound states and the positive energy pseudostates that discretize the continuum part of the spectrum (positive energies).
